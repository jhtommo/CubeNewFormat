Cube fm specialises in Water Treatment, Air Con, Electrical Planned & Reactive Maintenance Services. We are a ISO9001 compliant facilities management company our executive board members have over 40 years experience in the FM industry providing a consultative approach to our clients business to minimise unplanned downtime and maximise customer experience. We consider all the key drivers from the ground up and we’ll work with you to find the best solutions for your business. To support our clients and our 50 nationally based engineers we have a dedicated support desk team and emergency out of hours number. If you would like to find out more about our full products and services please email admin@cube-fm.co.uk and our team would be happy to help.
